About Ragdoll Cats

Origin

Ragdolls originated in California in the 1960s.

Temperament

They are gentle, affectionate, and often go limp when held.

Appearance

Ragdolls are large, have blue eyes, and soft semi-long fur.

Care

They require grooming twice a week and lots of cuddles.

Difference between Inline and Block Elements

  • Block-level: Starts on a new line and takes full width. Example: <div>, <p>, <h1>.
  • Inline: Flows within text and takes only required width. Example: <span>, <a>, <strong>.
